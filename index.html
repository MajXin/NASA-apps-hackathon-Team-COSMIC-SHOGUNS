About Water Pollution

Water pollution happens when harmful substances get into the water, making it dirty and dangerous. It's like adding yucky stuff to your favorite drink, making it not so tasty and healthy.

How Does Water Get Polluted???...

Water can get polluted in many ways. When people throw trash in rivers, lakes, or oceans, it's like making a mess in our play area. Chemicals from factories and homes can also find their way into the water, making it harmful for fish and other underwater friends.

Why Is Clean Water Important???...

Just like how you need a clean room to stay healthy and happy, fish and other water creatures need clean water to survive. Clean water is essential for plants and animals to live and grow. We must protect our water from pollution to keep everyone safe and healthy!

Effects of climate change on water cycle

So far, any impact that climate change may have had generally on regional rainfall cannot be distinguished from natural variations. However, for some specific cases a signal is starting to emerge. A recent study showed that man-made climate change substantially increased the odds of damaging floods occurring in England and Wales in autumn 2000.

The Water Cycle

Click for animated water cycle

The water cycle is a continuous process that describes the natural movement of water on, above, and below the surface of the Earth. It involves processes like evaporation, condensation, precipitation, and collection. This cycle is vital for maintaining life on our planet and ensuring a sustainable supply of water.
